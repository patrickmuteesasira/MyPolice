Crime Preventation

Crime prevention is the attempt to reduce and deter crime and criminals. It is applied specially to efforts made by government to reduce crime,enforce the law,and maintain criminal justice

Law Enforcement

Law enforcement is an umbrella term for the activities of some members of government who act in an organisated manner to enforce the law by discovering,deterring,rehabilating or punishing people who violate the rules and norms governing society.
